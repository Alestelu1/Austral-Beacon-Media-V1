About the Project

Austral Beacon Media is an independent editorial and geographic initiative focused on documenting the lighthouse network of southern Chile, including the Strait of Magellan, Cape Horn and the Chilean Antarctic territory.

The project combines cartography, field documentation, and digital storytelling to explore one of the most remote maritime regions on Earth.

It operates as the core brand behind the "End of the World Atlas" ecosystem.

Focus Areas

Strait of Magellan

Historic maritime route with strategic lighthouse infrastructure.

Cape Horn

Extreme navigation zone and symbolic endpoint of global exploration.

Chilean Antarctica

Scientific bases, navigation routes and polar logistics.

Connected Projects
